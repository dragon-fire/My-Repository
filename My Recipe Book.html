Recipe #1

  • Time: 10 mins
  • Serves: Chocolate Chip, peanut butter & Banana Sandwiches
Ingredients Quantity
creamy peanut butter 1/4 cup
honey 2 tablespoons
ground cinnamon 1/4 teaspoon
miniature semisweet chocolate chips 2 tablespoons
whole wheat bread 4 slices
a medium sized banana, thinly sliced 1 banana

Steps:

Step 1: Mix peanut butter, honey and cinnamon
Step 2: Stir in chocolate chips.
Step 3: Spread over bread.
Step 4: Layer two bread slices with banana slices.
Step 5: top with remaining bread.
Step 6 (optional): If desired, cut into shapes using cookie cutters.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 15 mins
  • Serves: Makeover Deluxe Grilled Cheese
Ingredients Quantity
a small onion, halved and thinly sliced 1 piece
French bread (1/2 inch thick) 4 slices
Butter-flavored cooking spray 1 spray
herbed fresh goat cheese 1 ounce
small tart apple, thinly sliced 1/2 piece
shredded reduced-fat cheddar cheese 1/2 cup

Steps:

Step 1: In a small skillet coated with cooking spray, saute onion until tender, set aside.
Step 2: Place bread slices on a baking sheet, spritz with butter-flavored cooking spray.
Step 3: Broil 4 in. from the heat until golden brown, 2-3 minutes.
Step 4: Spread goat cheese over 2 untoasted sides of bread slices.
Step 5: Top with apple slices and reserved onion, sprinkle with cheddar cheese.
Step 6: Broil until cheese is melted, 2-3 minutes longer. Top with remaining slices.

Recipe #3

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: Ham & Apple Grilled Cheese Sandwiches
Ingredients Quantity
Dijon mustard 2 tablespoons
honey 2 tablespoons
sourdough bread 4 slices
thinly sliced deli ham 4 ounces
thin slices apple 8 slices
shredded sharp cheddar cheese 1 cup
butter, softened 4-1/2 teaspoons

Steps:

Step 1: Combine mustard and honey, spread 2 tablespoons mixture over two bread slices.
Step 2: Layer with ham, apple and cheese.
Step 3: Spread remaining bread with remaining mustard mixture, place on top.
Step 4: Butter outsides of sandwiches.
Step 5: In a small skillet over medium heat, toast sandwiches for 2-3 minutes on each side or until cheese is melted.

Recipe #4

  • Time: 25 mins
  • Serves: Grilled Deli Sandwiches
Ingredients Quantity
a medium onion, sliced 1 piece
sliced fresh mushrooms 1 cup
julienned green pepper 1 cup
julienned sweet red pepper 1 cup
canola oil 2 tablespoons
sourdough bread 12 slices
thinly sliced deli ham, smoked turkey and pastrami 1/2 pound each
bacon strips, cooked and crumbled 6 strips
process American cheese 6 slices
Swiss cheese 6 slices

Steps:

Step 1: In a large skillet, saute the onion, mushrooms and peppers in oil until tender.
Step 2: Layer six bread slices with ham, turkey, pastrami, bacon, vegetables and cheese, top with remaining bread.
Step 3: Wrap each sandwich in foil.
Step 4: Grill, covered, over medium heat for 4-5 minutes on each side or until heated through.

Recipe #5

  • Time: 55 mins
  • Serves: Breakfast Burger
Ingredients Quantity
ground beef 1 pound
Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon
Montreal steak seasoning 1 teaspoon
salt, divided 1/2 teaspoon
pepper, divided 1/2 teaspoon
butter, softened and divided 3 tablespoons
Texas toast 8 slices
canola oil 2 tablespoons
frozen shredded hash brown potatoes, thawed 2-1/2 cups
large eggs 4 eggs
seedless blackberry spreadable fruit 1/4 cup
American cheese 4 slices
cooked bacon strips 8 strips

Steps:

Step 1: Combine ground beef, Worcestershire sauce, steak seasoning, 1/4 teaspoon salt and 1/4 teaspoon pepper, mixing lightly but thoroughly.
Step 2: Shape into four 1/2-in.-thick patties.
Step 3: Grill burgers, covered, on a greased grill rack over medium heat 4-5 minutes on each side until a thermometer reads 160°.
Step 4: Meanwhile, spread 2 tablespoons butter over one side of Texas toast slices, grill with burgers until golden brown. Remove burgers and toast from heat, keep warm.
Step 5: Increase heat to high. In a large skillet on grill rack, heat oil.
Step 6: Drop hash browns by 1/2 cupfuls into oil, press to flatten.
Step 7: Sprinkle with remaining salt and pepper.
Step 8: Fry, covered, until golden brown on each side and crisp, 12-15 minutes, adding oil as needed.
Step 9: Remove and keep warm.
Step 10: Reduce heat to medium.
Step 11: Add eggs, fry over easy.
Step 12: To assemble, spread blackberry preserves over four slices of Texas toast. Layer each slice with one hash brown patty, one burger, one fried egg, one cheese slice and two bacon strips. Top with remaining toast slices.
Step 13: To assemble, spread blackberry preserves over four slices of Texas toast.
Step 14: Layer each slice with one hash brown patty, one burger, one fried egg, one cheese slice and two bacon strips.
Step 15: Top with remaining toast slices.
